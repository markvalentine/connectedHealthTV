Name

  • Why aren’t my measurements showing up?

    In order for your bluetooth devices to transfer readings, they must be within 10 feet of your bluetooth receiver (located on the ROS box). Try taking your measurements closer to your ROS box, and if problems persist, call your care provider for assistance.

  • I’m out of lancets/strips for my glucometer, how do I order more?

    To order more lancets or strips for your glucometer, contact your care provider. They will provide you with any materials you may require!
